Often confused with dragons, drakes are of similar build to large dogs; four powerful legs and a strong jaw allow it to hold its ground as a proficient type. Some forms of media depict drakes with wings, distinguishing them from dragons by making them smaller and weaker. This mix of representation is the biggest reason why people don’t know that there is a difference between the two. The word drake originally was used to describe dragon-like creatures but had since gained enough popularity for it to become its own class.
